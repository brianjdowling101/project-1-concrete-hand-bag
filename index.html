Meet the Members

Alex (Vocals / Guitar)

Alex’s swirling guitar lines and soft-spoken vocals are the emotional backbone of Concrete Handbag. She brings melody to the noise.

Jordan (Bass)

Jordan lays down the gritty low-end textures that anchor the band’s sonic chaos. A minimalist with a noisy heart.

Ryan (Drums)

Ryan blends mechanical precision with improvised rhythms. A drummer who plays with silence as much as sound.

Lee (Guitar / Samples)

Lee manipulates loops, samples, and dissonance into dense atmospheres. Responsible for the dream-pop layer in the noise.

Music and Media

Concrete Handbag is a four-piece band combining elements of noise, post-punk, and dream pop. Formed in 2021, the group built its sound around layered guitars, lo-fi textures, and a shared interest in the tension between melody and distortion. Their music is shaped by city life — cold spaces, late nights, and overlooked corners — and explores themes of detachment, repetition, and quiet resistance. Drawing influence from underground scenes past and present, Concrete Handbag avoids nostalgia in favor of something more current, more unsettled. Their debut EP, Sidewalk Etiquette, was recorded on borrowed gear in a basement studio and released independently. Since then, they’ve played shows in DIY spaces and small venues, steadily growing a following through word of mouth and honest performances. Concrete Handbag is less about spectacle and more about mood — built on tone, texture, and the spaces in between.
